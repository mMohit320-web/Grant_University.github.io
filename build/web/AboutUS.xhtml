UNIVERSITY

Grant University, was established in the year 1976 with a specific objective-

"To establish and incorporate a teaching-cum-affliating University of California for the encouragement of interdisciplinary higher education research with special emphasis on studies of Life Sciences". During the last 26 years of its existance, the university has achieved a remarkable degree of success in its expansion programs, infrastructural developments, and academic excellence.

There are 66 boards of studies for developing and designing courses in different disciplines.

In addition to the main campus, there are two satellite campuses - Regional Centre running post-graduate teaching at California and National Law Collage at Texas.

As an affiliating University, it has 126 institutions and collages under its control with an enrolment of about 170,000 students.

Affiliating institutes of Grant University consist of a number of Engineering Collages, Computer and Management Studies institutes, and Pharmaceutical Collages.
